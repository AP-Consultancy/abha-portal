Place your Word template here as: BONAFIDE.docx

The app fills the blank lines in the school bonafide template automatically:
- Student name (after Ma./ Ku.)
- Father / guardian name (S/o / D/o line)
- Class
- Academic session (replaces the default session year in the template)
- Date of birth (DD/MM/YYYY)
- Scholar number

Do not rename the file. Keep the underscore blanks in the Word document as they are.
